About

Two students inspired by the beauty of nature

This website was created by Leah Kerry and Isabella Bock

We are two college students from the eastern and western side of PA. We met at Tufts University, where we are studying for our undergraduates in computer science. Our love for the environment and the natural beauty of Pennsylvania brought us together, and we hope we can share some of that passion with the world!

We plan to expand this website into a full guide to plants in our area and how to plant and care for them. Stay tuned for more updates!
